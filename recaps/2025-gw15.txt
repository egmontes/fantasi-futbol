Gameweek 15 Recap — Fantasí Fútbol

Summary 🌪️📊🍺

Gameweek 15 gave us everything: Harry’s long-awaited breakout, John King and Mark continuing their charge upward, Logan quietly cooking with galaxy-brain differentials, a bottom-two that didn’t actually play badly, and Dave now owing more Guinness than points he scored last week. Oh, and AFCON is about to supply the league with five free transfers, meaning the chaos meter is about to explode.

⸻

1️⃣ Harry Takes 1st — Bench Boost Bonanza 🧨🧠💙

@⁨Harry Wang⁩ finally escapes the cellar with his first top-4 finish of the year, landing in 1st place and throwing a rope up to the middle of the table.

He made 3 transfers that netted him +23 points, powered by:
	•	Foden’s 12-pointer
	•	Three clean sheets
	•	And a monstrous 28 points from his Bench Boost, almost all coming from two Everton bench players dropping 25 combined

A breakout week for Harry. It only took 15 gameweeks, 3 transfers, and Everton turning into 2004 AC Milan.

⸻

2️⃣ John King Finishes 2nd — Transfers Working Overtime 👑🔧⚽

@⁨John King⁩ earns 2nd place and his second top-4 finish of the year with a transfer masterclass.

His 4 transfers gave him +18 points, highlighted by:
	•	Bringing in Foden
	•	Bringing in Bowen (9 points)
	•	Three clean sheets
	•	And Bruno Fernandes dropping 18 points from two goals and an assist

John is now approaching the top half of the table, much to the league’s discomfort.

⸻

3️⃣ Mark Stays Hot in 3rd — Three Straight Top 4s 🔥🎩📈

@⁨Mark Wright⁩ lands 3rd place — his fourth top-4 finish and third in just three weeks.

He made no transfers, so he rolled with what he had:
	•	Foden
	•	Ndiaye (8 points)
	•	Most importantly, he captained Bruno Fernandes, doubling his 18 to a big 36-point haul

Mark continues his charge upward after a rough early season and now lurks near the top half of the table like a man reborn.

⸻

4️⃣ Logan Takes 4th — The Differential Dealer 🕵️‍♂️⚡🧩

@⁨Logan Kluis⁩ grabs his second top-4 finish with zero transfers and maximum vibes.

He hit on:
	•	Bruno Fernandes
	•	Mbuemo (goal)
	•	He was the only manager to start Rúben Dias (14 points)
	•	And the only manager to start Richarlison (8 points)

Logan continues his slow, methodical climb out of the basement. He might actually be good again.

⸻

5️⃣ Liam Cashman & Graham in the Bottom Two — First Shotguns 🍺😬⚠️

@⁨Liam Cashman⁩ and @⁨Graham Derfner⁩ finish at the bottom, earning their first Guinness shotguns of the season.
Deadline: Before 7:30am ET on Saturday, December 13th.

Liam had Foden, but then transferred out Guehi for a Sunderland defender, which was a brutal –7 point swing and basically sealed his fate.

Graham got nice returns from:
	•	Chalobah (11)
	•	Szoboszlai (8)
But the rest of his squad ghosted him.

Both remain in the top 10, but this week was definitely a speed bump.

⸻

@⁨Dave Bambrick⁩ failed to deliver last week’s required shotgun, meaning he now owes a double this Saturday morning.
