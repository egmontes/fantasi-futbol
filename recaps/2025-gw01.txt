🚨 Guinness at Sunrise & Bench Boost Madness: Gameweek 1 Recap 🚨

🏆 @⁨Liam Cashman⁩ – Cashmoney in 1st
Liam came flying out the gates like Ekitiké on steroids, grabbing the opening week crown with a massive 78 points. He nailed the captain’s armband on Ekitiké, who rewarded him with a monster 22 points. Add in Semenyo’s 15, plus Salah and Kudus chipping in, and Liam’s off to a dream start. For his audacity, Liam also takes home the very first Pacifico™️ Impulse Captain of the Week award, proving that sometimes recklessness pays.

🥈 @⁨Robert Shellow⁩ – The Ahsenal in 2nd
Robert is clearly trying to live up to his Arsenal-inspired team name: strong start, lots of hype, probably destined for heartbreak. With 72 points, he leaned on Semenyo (15) and Wood (13) while Saka wore the armband for a modest 6. He’s already top-four material, but will May treat him kindly? History says no.

🥉 @⁨Andrew Denucci⁩ – Ekitike-tembo & @⁨Collin Johnston⁩ – Fully Ars’d (Tied for 3rd)
Both Andrew and Collin finished on 71 points, sharing the bronze. Andrew went full chaos merchant, slamming his Bench Boost in WEEK 1 like a guy trying to win the lottery with scratch-offs. It worked this time, with Salah (16 captain) and Sánchez (8) padding his total. Collin, meanwhile, played it cool: Salah’s 16 and Wood’s 13 did the heavy lifting, and he matched Andrew without torching a chip. One’s reckless, one’s patient — only time will tell who wins the long game.

🍺 @⁨Ryan Kelly⁩ & @⁨Tommy Harris⁩ – Bottom Boys
Ryan scraped 36 points and Tommy managed 37, which means the pair of them are officially the league’s first Punishment Duo™️. Tomorrow morning, before most Americans have had coffee, these two legends will be shotgunning Guinness at 7:30am ET. Pray for their digestive systems — and their employers.
